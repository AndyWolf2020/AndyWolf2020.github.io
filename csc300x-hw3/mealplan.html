The Don

The gyoza that they serve are amazing. They are crispy and served with a sauce that pairs really well with it. The price is pretty standard for an appitizer at $8.
The chicken katsu curry is just like the one my mom makes back home. The chicken is crispy and the curry is flavorful. It always fills me up. It is priced at $13.45.
The spicy ramen is flavorful and comes with a variety of add-ons. It is very spicy so I would stick away from this if you have a hard time with spice. It is priced at $10.95.

Happy Bowl

The rice bowls come with a variety of toppings but the toppings that are really good are the fried egg, kimchi and scallions. For the main meat always get the bulgogi beef, it is tender and flavorful. The price of this bowl is about $11.95.
For a different rice bowl, get black beans with lettuce and tofu with their white sauce. This bowl comes out to about $7.95 and has as much flavor as the first one.
The hibachi is also really good if you would like something with less rice. The steak and shrimp hibachi is the best in my opinion and comes with vegetables and fried rice. The price of the hibachi plate is $10.75.

Don Gala Pizzaria

Da Butcher is a specialty pizza that has two different sizes or you can order just a slice. It comes with pepperoni, ham, sausage, bacon and beef for those meat lovers. The 18' is $28.50, the 12' is $16.50 and a slice is $6.50.
The classic cheese is $16.00 for the 18', $11.50 for the 12' and $4.00 for a slice.
Their cheesesteak comes with onions, peppers and mushrooms with american cheese. This is one of their best options costing $11.50.
